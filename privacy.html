BitRogue Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-21

BitRogue respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and what choices users have.

1. About the App

BitRogue is a turn-based trading game built around chart patterns, coin-specific news, and global market events. The app does not require account creation or sign-in.

2. Data Stored by the App

BitRogue may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, including:

  • game progress and current run state
  • upgrade point allocation and settings
  • language preference
  • other local save data needed for gameplay

This information is stored on the user’s device and is not directly collected by the developer.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

BitRogue may use Google AdMob to provide rewarded advertisements. In connection with advertising, Google and its partners may collect or process certain information.

Examples may include:

  • device identifiers
  • advertising data
  • app interaction data
  • diagnostic data
  • performance data
  • approximate location derived from IP address

For more information, please review Google’s policies: Google Privacy Policy and Google AdMob.

4. How Information May Be Used

  • to save game progress
  • to provide gameplay functionality
  • to show rewarded ads
  • to improve app performance and stability
  • to measure and optimize advertising performance

5. Directly Identifiable Information

BitRogue does not normally require or collect directly identifiable personal information such as a name, email address, or phone number. If you contact support directly, the information you provide may be used only to respond to your request.

6. Data Retention

Local save data may remain on your device until the app is deleted or the data is reset. Advertising-related or third-party service data is retained according to the policies of those providers.

7. Children’s Privacy

BitRogue does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If third-party advertising services are used, their own policies may apply.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Important changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

  • Email: your-email@example.com
  • Support page: BitRogue Support
